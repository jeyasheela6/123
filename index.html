Literary Structure and Aesthetics

1. Thirukkural:

Concept: Like a single, self-contained Kural couplet, its beauty and order are contained distinctly within itself. This is a clear structure that functions independently.

2. Silappatikaram:

Concept: Like the rules of a *Kandam* (Canto/Section), the style is defined at the beginning of the document and provides the same aesthetic to all internal sections.

3. Sangam Poems:

Concept: Like the general grammatical rules of *Akathinai* and *Purathinai* (love and war), this structure is derived externally, providing a common framework for thousands of poems.
